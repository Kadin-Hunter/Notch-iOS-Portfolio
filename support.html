Frequently asked questions

How do I add a counter?
Tap the + button on the home screen.
What does the "Full Customization" purchase unlock?
A one-time purchase unlocks unlimited counters, custom themes and appearance, custom accent colors, alternate app icons, haptics customization, step buttons, and countdown mode with alert patterns.
I bought Full Customization but features are still locked.
Open the app, go to Settings, and tap Restore Purchases. Purchases are tied to your Apple ID, so make sure you're signed in with the same account used to buy.
I reinstalled the app or got a new phone. Do I have to buy again?
No. Tap Restore Purchases in Settings — it re-unlocks at no charge on any device signed into the same Apple ID.
Where is my data stored?
Everything — counters and settings — is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded. Deleting the app deletes this data.
How do I request a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.
