# Privacy Policy — Notch

_Last updated: July 17, 2026_

Notch ("the app") is developed by Kadin Hunter. This policy explains how the
app handles your information. Short version: **the app does not collect,
transmit, or share any personal data.**

## Data we collect

**None.** Notch does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics or tracking SDKs.

## Data stored on your device

Your counters, settings, themes, and customization preferences are saved
**locally on your device** using Apple's standard on-device storage
(UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to
the developer. Deleting the app removes this data.

## In-app purchases

Notch offers a one-time in-app purchase ("Full Customization"). Purchases are
processed entirely by **Apple** through the App Store. The app does not see or
store your payment details. Purchase and restore are handled by Apple's
StoreKit; refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
for how Apple handles transaction data.

## Tracking

Notch does **not** track you across apps or websites and does not use the
Advertising Identifier (IDFA). There is no third-party advertising.

## Data sharing

Because no data is collected, **no data is shared** with the developer or any
third party.

## Children's privacy

Notch does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

Any updates will be posted at this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
